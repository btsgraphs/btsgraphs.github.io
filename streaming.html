Title Track Focused Playlists

Create 2 Title Track Focused Playlists on Spotify to maximize streams for title tracks and singles. You can also use this playlist format for Apple Music and other audio streaming apps, but Spotify is the only one that updates the number of streams for every song daily. This allows us to monitor our progress and set goals.

Add the title track or single every 3, 4, or 5 songs randomly.


Be strategic! Longevity is key.

If an old BTS album such as "Love Yourself: Answer" is still charting on official albums chart in your country, add at least 5 songs from that album to your latest playlists AND stream old BTS albums occasionally.

Although streams for old songs may not be enough for them to re-enter Spotify Charts, they will lead to more certifications and help stabilize positions on official albums charts. Streaming is a marathon, not a sprint.
